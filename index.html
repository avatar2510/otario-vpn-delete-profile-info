Account Deletion Request for OtarioVPN

You have the right to request the deletion of your OtarioVPN account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To initiate the account deletion process, please send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Email: otariovpn.info@gmail.com

Please send the request from the email address associated with the OtarioVPN account you wish to delete. This is necessary for us to verify your identity.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following personal data associated with your account:

  • Your account credentials (email address and hashed password).
  • Your subscription status and payment history provided by app stores.

What Data May Be Retained?

We do not store any logs of your online activity (browsing history, IP addresses, connection timestamps) as per our No-Logs Policy. Therefore, there is no such data to delete.

Anonymous and aggregated analytics data, which cannot be linked to your account, may be retained for statistical purposes.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will be notified by email once the process is complete.
